Tumhare Liye...

Par fir meri zindagi main aayi tum, tum auron se kuch alag lagi. Tumse baat karke kuch apna sa laga. Mai tumse attach nahi hona chahta tha par dheere dheere tumhari aadaton mein dhalne lage. Maine bahut samjhaya khud ko ki ek din tum bhi achanak mujhse door ho jaoge sabh ki tarah, raat bhar khoob baatein karoge aur subha hone tak sabh bhool jaoge.

Attachment buri cheez hoti hai mujhe pata tha par mai khud ko rok hi nahi paya. Pata nahi par andar se ek awaaz aati thi ki tumhare sath ye rishta lamba chalega kyoki tumhari taraf se bhi pyar dikhta tha, efforts dikhte the aur mai toh ye soch bhi nahi pa raha tha ki tum door chali jaoge toh kaisa lagega.

"Tumhare dil ko jitna main samajhta hoon, utna shayad tum khud bhi nahi samajhi. Main tumhe khone se nahi, tumhe hurt karne se darta hoon."
